🏃 What is Dreadhead Parkour?

Pro Tips for Mastering Dreadhead Parkour

🧱 Wall Climbing

If you just barely miss a jump and hit a solid wall, don't give up! Hold the forward key and press jump repeatedly to perform a wall-climb to pull yourself up the ledge.

⚖️ Land on Your Feet

If you are performing a flip, make sure you complete the rotation before hitting the ground. Landing on your head will instantly kill you and end the run.

🛑 Sliding Stops

If you are running too fast toward a cliff, initiating a slide creates friction and stops your character much faster than simply letting go of the run key.

⏳ Moving Platforms

Take your time on moving platforms. Let them complete a full cycle so you can observe the exact trajectory before you commit to the jump.

Tips and Strategies for Surviving the Course

  • Master the Slide: Sprinting is fast, but sliding is safer. Sliding drastically reduces your character's hitbox, allowing you to easily slip under low-hanging saw blades and laser beams without slowing down.
  • Flip for Points and Distance: Holding the left or right arrow keys while in the air performs a flip. Flips not only earn you extra coins for style, but a perfect forward flip actually extends your jump distance slightly.
  • Don't Rush the Bombs: When you trigger a red proximity mine, it takes a second to detonate. If you can't clear the blast radius in time, trigger it and immediately jump backward to let it explode before proceeding.
  • Hit the Checkpoints: Look for the green glowing TV screens. These are checkpoints. Make sure you physically touch them, so if you get chopped by a saw blade, you don't have to restart the entire level.
  • Hunt the Coins: Grabbing the coins scattered across the map is the only way to unlock the coolest alternative skins (like the zombie or the cyborg) in the main menu.
