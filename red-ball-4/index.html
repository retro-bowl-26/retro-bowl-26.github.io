🔴 What is Red Ball 4?

Red Ball 4 is a 2D side-scrolling puzzle platformer. The player controls a red ball that moves by rolling. The gameplay revolves around utilizing physics (momentum, gravity, buoyancy) to navigate over obstacles, push blocks to create stepping stones, and defeat square-shaped enemies by bouncing on their heads. Pro Tips for Mastering Red Ball 4

🌊 Water Physics

The Red Ball floats, but the wooden crates sink slightly when you stand on them. You have to jump quickly off floating crates before you sink into the deadly water.

⚡ The Sprint Jump

Holding the direction key while airborne allows you to carry your momentum further. If you stop pressing forward mid-air, you will drop straight down.

🧲 Cart Riding

When riding in minecarts, stand near the back of the cart when going downhill to prevent it from flipping over from your weight.

🛑 Beware the Spikes

Touching a spike or the side of an enemy instantly defeats you. If you miss a jump on an enemy, roll away immediately to try again.
