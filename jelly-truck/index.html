🚚 What is Jelly Truck?

Jelly Truck is a 2D side-scrolling physics racing game. The player controls a delivery truck made entirely of a gelatinous substance. The environment, including roads, blocks, and obstacles, is also made of jelly. The objective is to drive the truck from the start to the finish line of each level, utilizing the truck's ability to compress, stretch, and bounce to overcome physics-based puzzles.

Pro Tips for Mastering Jelly Truck

💥 Crush the Blocks

Smaller jelly boxes and spheres in your path can be rolled over and crushed. Don't be afraid to drive straight through them to clear the road.

🏗️ The Wheelie Hop

If you are stuck against a small ledge, lean back to do a wheelie, then quickly hit the gas to hop your front tires onto the ledge and drag yourself up.

🛑 Beware of Flipping

While your truck is squishy, landing completely upside down will usually leave you stranded. Always prioritize landing on your wheels after a big jump.

⏳ Let the Physics Settle

If a bridge or elevator is bouncing wildly, stop moving and wait a few seconds. The physics engine will eventually calm down, making it safe to cross.

Tips and Strategies for Wobbly Driving

  • Embrace the Squish: Unlike normal racing games, crashing isn't always bad. You can use your truck's squishy nature to squeeze through gaps that look way too small.
  • Control Your Lean: Balancing your truck mid-air is crucial. If you land upside down, your jelly truck can often get stuck. Lean forward or backward to land on your wheels.
  • Rock Back and Forth: If you get stuck in a dip or against a wall, alternate hitting the forward and reverse keys quickly. This causes your jelly truck to jiggle and can often pop you out of a trap.
  • Use the Environment: The world is made of jelly too! You can bounce off jelly trampolines, crush jelly blocks to create bridges, and ride moving jelly elevators.
  • Patience is Key: Don't just hold the gas pedal down. Some levels require you to stop completely and let the physics engine settle before attempting a tricky jump.
