Pro Tips for Mastering Level Devil

👀 Bait the Traps

Jump toward a platform and immediately pull back in mid-air to land back where you started. This "fake-out" jump will often trigger the trap, making it safe to cross on your next jump.

🧠 Memorize the Sequence

The traps are not random; they are hard-coded. Once you trigger a trap, it will always behave that exact same way on your next attempt. Memorize the sequence.

🚪 The Fake Door

Sometimes the door is a lie. If reaching the door kills you, look for a hidden path. The real exit might be hidden in the ceiling or in a seemingly deadly pit.

⬇️ Falling Hazards

If you are running down a long, empty hallway, don't feel safe. The ceiling is almost certainly going to drop on you. Be prepared to immediately reverse direction.

Tips and Strategies for Surviving the Trolls

  • Expect the Unexpected: If a jump looks too easy, it is a trap. Always assume the floor you are about to land on is going to disappear or shoot spikes at you.
