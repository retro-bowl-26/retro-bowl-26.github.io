🧟 What is Earn To Die?

Earn To Die is a 2D driving and upgrade game. The player drives a vehicle through a zombie-infested wasteland. The run ends when the vehicle runs out of fuel or gets completely stuck. The player is awarded cash based on the distance traveled and zombies destroyed. This cash is spent in a garage menu to upgrade the vehicle's fuel tank, engine, tires, transmission, and weaponry.

Pro Tips for Mastering Earn To Die

⛽ Fuel Management

When going down steep hills, you can briefly let off the accelerator to save a tiny bit of fuel while gravity does the work.

🛑 Avoid Crates

Smashing through wooden crates slows you down significantly. If you have a rocket boost, use it to blast straight through a wall of boxes without losing speed.

🛡️ Front Armor

Zombies actually slow your car down when you hit them. Buying a front bumper with spikes slices through them instantly, preserving your momentum.

🔄 The Daily Grind

Don't get frustrated if you only travel a few feet on your first run. The game is designed around grinding. Every run, no matter how short, earns you money.

Tips and Strategies for Maximum Upgrades

  • Gas is Everything: Early on, do not waste money on armor or guns. Buy a bigger fuel tank and a better engine. Distance is the primary way you earn cash, and you can't go far without gas.
  • Control Your Pitch: In the air, tilt your car so your back wheels hit the ground first. This ensures your drive wheels immediately catch traction, pushing you forward instead of bouncing.
  • Boost Wisely: When you buy a rocket booster, don't use it immediately. Save your boost for when your car gets bogged down by a massive pile of zombies or when trying to climb a steep hill.
  • Let the Gun Do the Work: Once you buy a mounted gun, it fires automatically. It clears the zombies in front of you, saving your car from losing momentum upon impact.
  • Buy the Next Car: Don't feel the need to fully max out the first hatchback. Save up and buy the pickup truck or the heavy truck as soon as possible, as their base stats are incredibly powerful.
