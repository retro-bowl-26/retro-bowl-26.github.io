Tips and Strategies for Arena Domination

  • Control Your Spin: You automatically move in the direction you are facing. Tapping your button lightly alters your spin; holding it makes you jump high. Master this to control your trajectory.
  • Grab the Weapons: Boxes drop randomly from the sky containing swords, bombs, and laser guns. Prioritize grabbing these over stomping, as they give a massive advantage.
  • Play Possum: In a 4-player game, let the other three players fight it out. Stay in the corners and avoid the crossfire until only one weakened opponent remains.
  • Watch for Hazards: The arenas have environmental traps like spikes and lava. Sometimes it's better to push an opponent into a trap than risk jumping on them.
