🐷 What is Bacon May Die?

Bacon May Die is an endless side-scrolling beat 'em up arcade game. The player controls a pig fighting off waves of enemies. The combat is uniquely tied to movement: running into enemies initiates melee attacks, while holding direction buttons fires ranged weapons. Pro Tips for Mastering Bacon May Die

⚔️ The Uppercut Combo

Pressing Up when next to an enemy launches them into the air. Follow up with quick forward taps to juggle them helplessly.

🛡️ Deflect Projectiles

You can actually hit enemy projectiles back at them with a well-timed melee swing. This is crucial for surviving bomber enemies.

💥 Crowd Control

If you are completely surrounded, jump straight up and immediately press down. This performs a ground slam that knocks nearby enemies away.

🔫 Ammo Management

Your gun doesn't have infinite rapid-fire. Watch the small reload circle over your character. Swap to melee while your gun cools down.

Tips and Strategies for Surviving Longer

  • Master the Dodge: Swiping down or pressing down while in the air initiates a powerful dodge-roll. This gives you invincibility frames to escape being surrounded.
  • Mix Melee and Ranged: Don't just rely on your sword or bat. Keep your distance from heavy enemies by holding the forward button to shoot your ranged weapon.
  • Use Slow Motion: When you perform a dodge or jump over projectiles, the game briefly enters slow motion. Use this window to land guaranteed critical hits on dangerous targets.
  • Watch the Skies: Enemies don't just come from the sides; some will drop from the top of the screen or throw bombs. Always keep an eye on the airspace above your pig.
  • Unlock Better Gear: Survive longer to earn coins. Spend these coins in the shop between runs to buy weapons with better range and damage stats, making future runs much easier.
