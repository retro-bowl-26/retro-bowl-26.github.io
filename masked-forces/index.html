Pro Tips for Mastering Masked Forces

💥 Burst Fire

When using assault rifles at medium to long range, do not hold the trigger down. Fire in short 2-3 round bursts to keep your crosshair accurate.

🏃 Reload in Cover

Never reload while standing in the open. Always sprint behind a wall or crate, reload your weapon, and then pop back out to re-engage the enemy.

🔪 The Knife Switch

You run slightly faster when holding your knife. If you need to cross a large open area quickly, switch to your melee weapon, then switch back to your gun before turning a corner.

🎯 Pre-Aim Corners

As you turn a corner, keep your crosshair exactly at head-height where you expect an enemy to be. This eliminates the time needed to adjust your aim.

  • Upgrade Your Armor: It's tempting to spend all your cash on a cool sniper rifle, but upgrading your body armor will drastically increase your survivability in close-quarters gunfights.
