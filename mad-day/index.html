🐙 What is Mad Day?

Mad Day is a 2D action-racing upgrade game. The player's truck drives forward automatically. The player must use the keyboard to make the truck jump over gaps and obstacles, while simultaneously pressing a shoot button to fire roof-mounted weapons at alien enemies. Collecting coins during the run allows the player to purchase significant upgrades in the garage to survive longer on the next attempt.

Pro Tips for Mastering Mad Day

🚀 The Double Jump

Upgrading your vehicle to have a Double Jump is a game-changer. It allows you to reach high platforms filled with coins and easily dodge low-flying alien ships.

🛡️ Stomp the Aliens

If you don't have enough firepower to shoot an alien on the ground, landing your heavy truck directly on top of them will squish them instantly.

💥 Rocket Launcher

Once you unlock the rocket launcher, use it! It fires automatically and deals massive splash damage to flying saucers that are hard to hit with your regular gun.

💰 The Spin Wheel

Never skip the spin wheel if you have tokens. Winning a temporary 'Coin Doubler' before a run will drastically reduce the time it takes to grind for the best upgrades.

Tips and Strategies for Beating the Aliens

  • Jump and Shoot: Your truck drives forward automatically. Your main job is to jump over obstacles and shoot enemies. You can do both simultaneously! Always keep firing when enemies are on screen.
  • Upgrade the Gun First: While armor and health are important, increasing your gun damage is the fastest way to progress. Dead aliens can't shoot back.
  • Collect the Spinners: Grab the floating spinning tokens during your run. These are used to play the mini-game spinner between levels, granting you massive coin bonuses or temporary power-ups.
  • Watch the Lasers: Alien spaceships will telegraph their laser attacks with a thin red line. Make sure you jump (or stay grounded) to avoid being in that line when the laser fires.
  • Equip Gadgets: Don't forget the 'Gadgets' tab in the shop. Items like the Magnet (which pulls coins to you) drastically increase the amount of money you earn per run.
