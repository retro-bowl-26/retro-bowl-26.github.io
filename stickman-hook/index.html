🕸️ What is Stickman Hook?

Stickman Hook is an arcade skill game based on swinging physics. The player controls a character who can shoot a grappling hook to connect to designated circular anchor points. While attached, the character swings like a pendulum. Releasing the hook launches the character through the air. The goal is to navigate through an obstacle course and cross a checkered finish line without falling out of bounds.

Pro Tips for Mastering Stickman Hook

⏱️ Timing the Release

For maximum distance, release the grapple when your character is at the lowest point of the swing (the bottom of the arc), right as they start to swing upward.

🧱 Wall Clinging

If you hit a solid wall, you don't instantly lose. Sometimes you can rapidly tap the grapple button to slowly climb up the wall or save yourself from falling.

🚀 The Slingshot Effect

If you grapple a peg that is directly behind you while you are moving forward quickly, it acts like a tight rubber band and slingshots you forward with massive speed.

💃 Dance at the End

Once you cross the finish line, your character performs a funny dance. The more skins you unlock, the more unique and hilarious dances you get to see.

Tips and Strategies for Perfect Swings

  • Momentum is Everything: The faster you are moving when you attach your hook, the wider and faster your swing will be. Try to fall a bit before grappling to build speed.
  • Don't Hook Everything: Just because there is a peg doesn't mean you need to grapple it. Often, carrying your momentum past multiple pegs is the fastest and safest route.
  • Trust the Bounce: Trampolines and bounce pads will automatically propel you forward. You don't need to grapple while bouncing; let the pads do the work until you reach an empty gap.
  • Short vs. Long Swings: A quick tap of the grapple button allows you to perform a short, tight swing to get over a small wall. Holding it down creates a massive pendulum swing for long jumps.
  • Skip the Hard Parts: With enough momentum from a high bounce, you can literally fly over the top of the screen, completely skipping complex mazes of traps below.
