🏰 What is Age of War?

Age of War is a 2D side-scrolling strategy game. Your base is on the left, the enemy is on the right. You spend gold to train troops who automatically march right to fight. When units die, you gain gold and XP. Enough XP lets you advance to the next historical era, unlocking better troops and stronger base turrets.

  • Five distinct ages (Stone, Castle, Renaissance, Modern, Future)
  • Dozens of unique unit types
  • Upgradable base defenses
  • Epic, nostalgic soundtrack
