🚜 What is Hills Of Steel?

Hills Of Steel is a 2D side-scrolling action game. Players control a tank moving across hilly, physics-based terrain. The objective is to destroy enemy tanks, jeeps, and helicopters while surviving as long as possible. The tank's cannon is fixed, meaning players must tilt their vehicle using the hills to aim their shots. Defeating enemies rewards coins, which are used to upgrade the tank or buy entirely new vehicles.

Pro Tips for Mastering Hills Of Steel

💥 Ramming Speed

If you completely run out of options, driving your tank directly on top of smaller enemy vehicles will crush them, dealing massive damage.

🚁 Anti-Air Tactics

Helicopters are annoying. Drive backward up a steep slope to point your cannon straight into the sky, then unload your shells before they drop bombs on you.

⚖️ Don't Flip Over

Driving too fast over sharp peaks will cause your tank to flip upside down. This exposes your weak underbelly and makes you unable to shoot. Control your speed!

💰 Grind Early Levels

If a boss is too hard, don't get frustrated. Replay the easier early levels to farm coins, upgrade your armor, and try the boss again with a stronger tank.

Tips and Strategies for Tank Domination

  • Angle With the Terrain: You cannot aim your cannon up or down manually. You must drive up a hill to aim higher, or drive down a slope to aim lower. Master using the environment to aim.
  • Reverse and Fire: Don't just charge forward. When an enemy fires, throw your tank into reverse to dodge the shell, then creep forward to launch your counter-attack.
  • Upgrade the Gun First: While armor is nice, killing enemies before they can shoot you is the best defense. Spend your coins on upgrading your gun damage early in the game.
  • Unlock the Joker: The starting "Cobra" tank is good, but saving up for the "Joker" (which shoots bouncing shells) or the "Titan" makes surviving the later levels much easier.
  • Prepare for Bosses: Every few waves, a massive boss vehicle will appear. Stay at maximum range and chip away at their health. Do not let them run you over!
