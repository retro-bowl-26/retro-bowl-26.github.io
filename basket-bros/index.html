Pro Tips for Mastering Basket Bros

🛡️ The Pump Fake

Tap the shoot button lightly while on the ground to perform a pump fake. This often tricks AI and human opponents into jumping, giving you a clear lane to the basket.

👟 Unlock Better Gear

Don't ignore the cosmetics! Unlocking and equipping better shoes actually gives your character a slight boost to their speed or jump height.

💥 Elbows Out

If you don't have the ball, mash the action button when near the opponent. Knocking them to the floor guarantees a turnover and an easy fast-break.

🎯 Three-Point Range

Shooting from beyond the arc requires holding the shoot button to fill a power meter. Practice the timing in the warm-up before trying it in a tight game.

Tips and Strategies for Winning the Tournament

  • Master the Dunk: Three-pointers are risky. The safest way to score is to drive into the paint, jump, and press the throw button right at the apex of your jump to trigger an unstoppable dunk animation.
  • Defensive Swiping: When playing defense, don't just stand there. Aggressively swipe at the opponent using the attack button to knock them down and steal the ball. There are no fouls!
  • Time Your Blocks: If an opponent is going for a jump shot or a dunk, wait for them to leave the ground before you jump. A well-timed block will send the ball flying across the court.
  • Know Your Bro: Every character has different stats. Some are incredibly fast but weak, while others are slow but impossible to block. Pick a Bro that matches your playstyle.
  • Use the Dash: Double-tapping a direction lets you dash. Use this to quickly close the gap on defense or blow past a defender on offense.
