Pro Tips for Mastering Moto X3M

⚖️ Land Smoothly

Landing flat on both wheels at the exact angle of the ramp ensures you don't lose any speed. A bumpy landing costs precious fractions of a second.

🔄 Double Flips

Off massive jumps, try to squeeze in two flips instead of one. This deducts a full 1.0 second from your time, which is massive for your rating.

🛑 Stop and Drop

Some levels require you to stop completely and let gravity drop you into a vertical shaft. Don't hold the gas when falling straight down.

💥 Use Explosions

Sometimes hitting a TNT barrel is required. The blast wave will propel your bike forward across massive gaps if angled correctly.

Tips and Strategies for 3-Star Times

  • Flip for Time: Every time you successfully complete a frontflip or backflip in mid-air, 0.5 seconds is deducted from your final time. Flips are mandatory for getting 3 stars on later levels.
  • Control Your Pitch: Don't just hold the gas. Use the left and right arrow keys to adjust your bike's pitch in the air to ensure you land perfectly parallel to the ground to maintain momentum.
  • Brake Wisely: Going full speed into a blind drop is usually a death sentence. Use the brakes to navigate tight corners or carefully roll onto a moving platform.
  • Lean on Climbs: When going up a steep hill, lean your rider forward to prevent the bike from doing an accidental wheelie and flipping over backwards.
  • Memorize the Traps: You will die a lot. Use the instant respawn feature at checkpoints to memorize the timing of swinging pendulums and crushing blocks.
