🐱 What is Cat Trap?

Cat Trap is a strategic puzzle game played on a board of hexagonal tiles. The player and the cat take turns. The player clicks a light tile to turn it into a dark wall, and the cat moves one tile toward the edge. The game ends when the cat is completely enclosed by walls (Player wins) or when the cat steps off the edge of the board (Cat wins).

Pro Tips for Mastering Cat Trap

🕸️ The Horseshoe Strategy

Build a 'U' shape of walls far ahead of the cat's path. Once it walks into the 'U', you can easily close the open end.

❌ Don't Block Directly

Blocking the hex directly in front of the cat gives it an immediate chance to step sideways. Block the hexes *next* to its future path.

🧩 Analyze the Start

Before making your first click, scan the board. Look for clusters of dark hexes and plan your walls to link those clusters together.

🧠 Be Patient

The cat's moves are entirely dictated by your placement. Take your time to trace the cat's guaranteed escape route before clicking.

Tips and Strategies for Trapping the Cat

  • Build a Wide Net: Do not place your first walls right next to the cat. Start by building a wide perimeter a few hexes away to cut off its long-term escape routes.
  • Connect the Dots: Every board starts with a few randomly placed dark hexes. Use these to your advantage by building lines connecting them together.
  • Funnel the Feline: Intentionally leave one side open to trick the cat into walking that way, while you secretly build a wall behind it.
  • Anticipate the AI: The cat is smart and will always take the shortest open path to the edge. Think two steps ahead before you click.
  • Don't Chase: If you constantly click the hex directly in front of the cat, it will easily zigzag around you and escape. Play defensively.
