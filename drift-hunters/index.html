Pro Tips for Mastering Drift Hunters

⚙️ Negative Camber

In the tuning menu, add negative camber to your front wheels. This gives you better steering response and stability while sliding sideways.

🗺️ Best Track for Cash

The 'Docks' track is wide open with massive sweeping corners. It is arguably the easiest track to string together a massive 10x multiplier to farm cash quickly.

🕹️ Manual Transmission

For true drifting mastery, switch to Manual transmission in the settings. Holding the car in 3rd gear prevents the auto-gearbox from shifting up and ruining your wheel spin.

🛠️ Weight Reduction

Before buying an expensive turbo, invest in weight reduction. A lighter car transitions between corners much faster and requires less horsepower to break traction.

Tips and Strategies for Massive Combos

  • Feather the Throttle: Don't just hold the gas down 100% of the time. While in a drift, repeatedly tap the gas (feathering) to maintain your angle without spinning out.
  • Chain Your Drifts: To get a massive multiplier, you must link drifts together. As you exit one corner, instantly flick your steering to the opposite side to transition into the next drift before your combo timer expires.
  • Use the E-Brake: The handbrake (Spacebar) is your best friend. Tapping it at high speeds initiates an aggressive drift instantly, allowing you to enter tight corners sideways.
  • Tune Your Gearbox: In the tuning menu, adjust your gears. You want a car that can bounce off the rev-limiter in 3rd or 4th gear to keep the rear wheels spinning through long corners.
  • Upgrade the Turbo: Upgrading your engine and turbo first gives you the raw horsepower needed to maintain drifts on the wider, faster tracks like the Docks.
