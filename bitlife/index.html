🧬 What is BitLife?

BitLife is a text-based life simulation game. The player lives a life from birth to death, pressing an 'Age' button to advance a year. During each year, the player can interact with menus to study, work, date, commit crimes, or buy assets. The game generates random events requiring moral or strategic choices. The ultimate goal is simply to experience different life paths and collect achievement ribbons upon death.

Pro Tips for Mastering BitLife

💄 Improving Looks

If you are born with low Looks, don't panic. Once you turn 18 and get a job, you can use the Plastic Surgery menu to boost your stats (but beware of botched surgeries!).

💰 The Lottery Strategy

👨‍👩‍👧 Generational Wealth

If you have a child and leave all your assets to them in your will, you can choose to "continue as child" when you die, allowing you to build massive multi-generational wealth.

🚔 Prison Escapes

If you end up in prison, escaping triggers a difficult mini-game where the guard moves twice for every move you make. You must trap the guard behind walls to reach the exit.

Tips and Strategies for a Successful Virtual Life

  • Boost Your Smarts Early: As a child and teenager, constantly use the 'Study Harder' option in school and read books at the library. High smarts unlock lucrative careers and university scholarships.
  • Maintain Your Health: Go to the gym, meditate, and visit the doctor immediately if you catch an illness. Ignoring your health will lead to a very sudden "Game Over."
  • Choose Careers Wisely: Not all jobs pay the same. Becoming an actor or a brain surgeon pays millions, but requires very high Looks or Smarts respectively. Plan your education around your target career.
  • Invest in Real Estate: Once you get a good job, don't just hold cash. Buy houses! Real estate generally appreciates in value over time, making it the easiest way to become a virtual millionaire.
  • Avoid Unnecessary Risks: Getting into bar fights, arguing with police, or gambling heavily are easy ways to ruin a perfectly good life. Think twice before picking the aggressive dialogue options.
