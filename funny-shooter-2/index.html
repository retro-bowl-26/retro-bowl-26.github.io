Tips and Strategies for Arena Survival

  • Keep Moving: The arena is huge. Never stand still. Sprint backward in a wide circle to herd the enemies into a tight group, making them easy targets for explosives.
  • Aim for the Head: Headshots deal massive critical damage. Taking the time to aim slightly higher will save you tons of ammunition and drop enemies much faster.
  • Buy the RPG Early: While assault rifles are fun, saving your early coins for an explosive weapon like the RPG or Grenade Launcher makes dealing with large crowds infinitely easier.
  • Watch the Minimap: Enemies can spawn from anywhere. Constantly check the radar in the corner of your screen to ensure nobody is sneaking up behind you with a giant frying pan.
  • Use the Environment: Jump on top of crates, buildings, and walls. Most melee-focused enemies cannot reach you if you take the high ground, giving you a safe place to reload.
