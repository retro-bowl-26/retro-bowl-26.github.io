Frequently Asked Questions

I requested a game. When will it be added?

We update the site every week. If a requested game is HTML5 compatible and safe, it usually takes 3-5 days to appear on the site.

Why is a game showing a blank screen?

Your school's firewall may have blocked the specific iframe server. Please report the exact game to us so we can update the proxy link!

Can I lose my save data?

Yes. Game saves are stored locally in your browser cache. If you clear your history or use incognito mode, your progress will be lost.

Are these games safe from viruses?

Absolutely. We rigorously screen all embedded content to ensure there are no malicious scripts or shady pop-up redirects.
