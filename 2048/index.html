🧩 What is 2048?

2048 is a single-player sliding block puzzle game. Played on a simple 4x4 grid, the player must slide numbered tiles to combine them. Every turn, a new tile randomly appears with a value of either 2 or 4. The game ends when the board fills up and no more moves are possible.

Pro Tips for Mastering 2048

🧱 Build a Chain

Try to line up your tiles in descending order leading to your highest tile. This allows you to collapse them sequentially.

🛑 Avoid the Up Arrow

If you are keeping your largest tile at the bottom, pressing 'Up' will pull it away from the corner, often ruining your setup.

♾️ Go Beyond 2048

Once you reach the 2048 tile, the game doesn't force you to stop. You can keep playing to reach 4096 or even 8192.

⏳ Think Two Steps Ahead

Treat the board like a chess game. A quick, uncalculated swipe can trap your large tiles with low-value numbers forever.

Tips and Strategies for High Scores

  • The Corner Strategy: Keep your highest value tile in one specific corner (e.g., bottom-right) and never move it out of that position.
  • Limit Your Swipes: Stick to swiping in only three directions. If your highest tile is in the bottom-right, only swipe Down, Right, and Left.
  • Build a Chain: Keep your numbers in descending order next to your highest tile, allowing for easy chain-reaction merges.
  • Plan Ahead: Don't just swipe randomly. Anticipate where the new '2' or '4' tile will spawn after your move.
  • Stay Calm: When the board gets crowded, one wrong move can ruin your game. Take your time to find the safest swipe.
