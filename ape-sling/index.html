🐒 What is Ape Sling?

Ape Sling is a vertical climbing arcade game. The player character hangs from circular pegs. By dragging away from the peg, the player creates a slingshot trajectory line. Releasing the drag launches the character toward the next peg. Missing a peg, falling out of the bottom of the screen, or hitting a trap results in a game over.

Pro Tips for Mastering Ape Sling

📐 Use the Trajectory Line

The dotted line shows exactly where your character will fly. Always double-check that the end of the line touches the blue center of the next peg.

🧱 Wall Gliding

If you undershoot your aim, sometimes hitting the side wall will slide your character down directly onto a lower peg, saving your run.

⏳ Don't Rush the Blue Pegs

Standard blue pegs are completely safe. You can hang from them forever. Take your time to study the moving traps above you before launching.

⚡ Rapid Fire on Red

When you land on a red breaking peg, don't try to aim perfectly. A quick, less accurate shot to the wall is better than falling.

Tips and Strategies for High Scores

  • Pull Back Far: The further you pull back, the more power your sling has. A powerful shot is less affected by gravity and travels in a straighter line.
  • Watch the Bounce: You can bounce off the side walls to reach tricky pegs. Use this to bypass traps blocking your direct path upward.
  • Time the Moving Saws: Be patient. If a buzzsaw is moving between you and the next peg, wait for it to pass before releasing your sling.
  • Avoid Red Pegs: Red anchor points are fragile and will break shortly after you grab them. If you land on one, you must sling again immediately!
