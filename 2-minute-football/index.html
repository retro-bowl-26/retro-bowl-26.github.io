🏈 What is 2 Minute Football?

2 Minute Football is a top-down American football arcade game focused exclusively on offense. The player controls the Quarterback. After snapping the ball, the player must survey the field and click to throw the ball to a receiver. Completing passes gains yards, and the goal is to score as many touchdowns as possible within a strict two-minute time limit.

Pro Tips for Mastering 2 Minute Football

📏 Master the 'Lead'

The ball takes time to travel in the air. Always click 2-3 yards ahead of where your receiver is currently running to ensure a clean catch.

🛑 Avoid Interceptions

An interception instantly ruins your drive and wastes precious clock. If a defender is standing between you and the receiver, do not throw it.

🏃 Yards After Catch

The play isn't over when the ball is caught. Use your mouse to weave your receiver left and right to dodge the safeties and break for a huge touchdown.

⏱️ Quick Slants

Short, quick passes over the middle of the field are the safest way to guarantee yards and keep the chains moving without risking a sack.

Tips and Strategies for Throwing Touchdowns

  • Lead Your Receivers: Do not throw the ball directly at your receiver's current position. You must click ahead of them so the ball and the receiver arrive at the same spot simultaneously.
  • Read the Defense: Before you snap the ball, look at how the defenders are positioned. If a receiver is double-covered, do not force the throw! Look for the open man.
  • Watch the Pass Rush: You only have a few seconds in the pocket before the defensive line breaks through and sacks you. Make your read quickly.
  • Don't Be Afraid to Run: If all your receivers are covered and the pocket is collapsing, scramble! Running for a short gain is much better than taking a sack and losing time.
  • Manage the Clock: Incomplete passes stop the clock. If you are running out of time and need a big play, throwing it away is safer than taking a tackle inbounds.
