🧩 What is Bloxorz?

Bloxorz is an isometric puzzle game focused on spatial reasoning. The player navigates a 1x2 rectangular block across floating platforms. The goal is to maneuver the block so it falls vertically into a 1x1 square hole at the end of the stage. The game introduces various mechanics as it progresses, such as bridges triggered by weight switches, fragile tiles that break under vertical pressure, and teleporters that split the block in half.

  • Spatial grid-based movement

  • Level passcode system

🎮 How to Play

  • ↑ ↓ ← → Use arrow keys to roll the block in that direction
  • Spacebar Switch between block halves (when split by a teleporter)
  • O / X Roll over circle (O) or cross (X) switches to build bridges
  • ⚠️ Do not stand vertically on orange fragile tiles

Pro Tips for Mastering Bloxorz

📝 Save Your Codes

At the top right of every level, a 6-digit code is displayed. This is your save file! Type this code in the main menu's 'Load Stage' screen to skip ahead.

🌉 Bridge Toggling

Pay attention to switches. Some switches build bridges, while others destroy them. Hitting the same switch twice might trap you!

✂️ Split Personality

When your block splits into two 1x1 cubes via a parenthesis switch "( )", remember that the two cubes cannot fall through the exit hole. You must join them back together first.

🔄 Patience is Key

There is no time limit. Moving too fast often leads to rolling off the edge. Carefully plan your 90-degree turns before making them.

Tips and Strategies for Solving Levels

  • Think in 3D: Your block takes up 2 tiles when lying flat, and 1 tile when standing vertically. You must perfectly align your block to be standing vertically directly over the square hole to win.
  • Watch the Fragile Tiles: Orange tiles are fragile. If you stand your block vertically on an orange tile, it will crash through and you will lose. You must roll over them while lying flat.
  • Understand the Switches: Circle switches are soft; they activate when you roll over them in any position. "X" switches are hard; they only activate when your block is standing vertically directly on top of them.
  • Work Backwards: If you are stuck on a hard level, look at the exit hole. Figure out which tiles you need to be standing on to drop in, and trace that path backward to your current position.
  • Write Down Passcodes: Every time you beat a level, the game gives you a 6-digit code. Write it down! If you accidentally close your browser, you can type this code in the main menu to skip the levels you already beat.
