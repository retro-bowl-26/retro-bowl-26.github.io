🍄 What is Super Mario Bros?

Super Mario Bros is a 2D side-scrolling platformer originally released for the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES). The player navigates Mario across linearly progressing levels, moving from left to right. The primary mechanic is jumping to defeat enemies, clear gaps, and hit blocks to reveal coins and power-ups. The game is divided into 8 'Worlds', each containing 4 sub-levels, ending with a castle boss fight.

Pro Tips for Mastering Super Mario Bros

⭐ The Starman

If you find a glowing star, grab it immediately! It grants you temporary invincibility, allowing you to sprint through enemies to instantly defeat them and rack up massive points.

🐢 Kick the Shells

When you jump on a Koopa Troopa, it retreats into its shell. Walk into the shell to kick it forward. It will obliterate any enemies in its path, but beware—it can bounce off pipes and hit you!

⏱️ Watch the Timer

Every level has a strict time limit. If the timer reaches 0, you lose a life regardless of your health. Don't spend too much time hunting for secrets.

🏰 Castle Mazes

In Worlds 4-4 and 7-4, the castles are repeating mazes. If you don't take the correct upper, middle, or lower path, the level will loop endlessly until time runs out.
