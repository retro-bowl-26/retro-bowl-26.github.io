🤸 What is Flip Bros?

Flip Bros is a 2D physics-based action game. The player controls a character who cannot walk normally. Instead, pressing and holding the action button makes the character jump and spin rapidly in mid-air. Letting go of the button launches the character forward. The goal is to calculate the perfect trajectory and momentum to crash into enemy characters and knock them off the screen.

Pro Tips for Mastering Flip Bros

🎯 The Trajectory Line

Pay close attention to your character's body alignment while spinning. You will shoot exactly in a straight line from your character's feet to their head the moment you release.

🧱 Domino Effect

If enemies are standing close together, hitting one with enough speed will cause them to fly into the other, earning you a cool double-knockout.

Your character is bouncy! If you aim at a solid wall above an enemy, you can ricochet off the wall and drop directly onto their head from above.

Some levels feature TNT crates. Hitting the TNT is much easier than hitting a small enemy, and the resulting explosion will clear the whole room.

Tips and Strategies for Perfect Hits

  • Master the Hold and Release: Holding the mouse button makes your character spin rapidly in the air, storing energy. Releasing the button shoots you in the direction you are currently facing. Timing the release is everything!
  • Use the Environment: You don't always need a direct hit. You can launch yourself into a stack of boxes to make them crush the enemy, or hit a hanging platform to drop it on their head.

  • Watch for Traps: Spikes and bottomless pits will instantly end your run. Make sure your trajectory doesn't send you flying over the enemy and into a hazard.
