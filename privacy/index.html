Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Effective Date: February 20, 2026 | Version 2.2

1. Introduction

Your privacy is incredibly important to us at Retro Bowl 26 ("we," "our," or "us"). Because our primary audience includes students playing unblocked games on school networks, we have designed our platform to collect as little personal data as possible. This Privacy Policy details what information is collected, how it is used, and how we keep it safe.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Unlike many other gaming platforms, we pride ourselves on being a no-registration website. Therefore, we do not ask for or collect:

  • Names, physical addresses, or phone numbers
  • Account passwords (we have no user accounts)
  • Financial or payment information

3. Information We Do Collect

3.1. Automatically Collected Technical Data

To keep the website running smoothly and fix bugs, our servers and analytics tools automatically collect basic technical information, including:

  • Device Data: Browser type (Chrome, Safari), operating system (ChromeOS, Windows), and generalized device type.
  • Network Data: Masked or anonymized IP addresses to prevent DDoS attacks.
  • Usage Metrics: Which games are played the most, page load times, and duration of visits.

3.2. Information You Provide Voluntarily

If you use our "Contact Us" form to request a game or report a bug, we will collect the email address and name you provide solely for the purpose of replying to your message. We delete these emails periodically.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

Retro Bowl 26 uses Cookies and HTML5 Local Storage for two main purposes:

  • Game Saves: Many HTML5 games (like Retro Bowl or Cookie Clicker) use your browser's Local Storage to save your game progress. If you clear your browser data, your game saves will be deleted. We do not store this data on our servers.
  • Analytics & Ads: We use third-party cookies (via Google Analytics and Google AdSense) to measure website traffic and deliver non-intrusive advertisements.

5. Third-Party Advertisers

We use Google AdSense to fund our free hosting. Google and its partners use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the Internet. Users may opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

6. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

We are fully compliant with the requirements of COPPA. Our website does not knowingly collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) from anyone under 13 years of age. Our website, products, and services are all directed to people who are at least 13 years old or older. If we discover that a child under 13 has submitted an email address via our contact form, we will delete it immediately.

7. Data Security

All traffic between your browser and our website is encrypted using standard SSL/HTTPS protocols. This ensures that your connection is secure, which is especially important when bypassing restrictive school firewalls.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Effective Date."

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact our privacy team at: privacy@retro-bowl-26.github.io
